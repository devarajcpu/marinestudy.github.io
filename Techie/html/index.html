Hello pc and laptop users..!! Are you using mobile hotspot to connect to your laptop or pc. In that case, you must pay attention to following points. Else within few minutes you will recieve a text saying Dear user you have consumed your daily limit. Please recharge with alternative plan. But Don't Worry..!! We are here to help you out to save your data. So let's get in..!!

The reasons are:

  • Auto Update of the windows
  • Running Background Apps
  • Microsoft Store auto update
  • Live Tiles
  • Few other settings related to network

Follow following steps to save your data by minimizing the data consumed by your pc or laptop

1.Turn Off Auto Update:

  1. Open Control Panel and Click on Administrive Tools
  2. Select Service
  3. Scroll Down to Windows Update
  4. It'll show Automatic. So first click on Stop
  5. Next drag down and select Disabled
  6. Click on Start
  7. Click Ok

2.Network Settings

  1. After connecting to mobile hotspot. Go to Network & Settings
  2. Click on Wi-fi
  3. Click on connected device
  4. Scroll Down to Metered Connection and turn it On

3.Microsoft Store Settings

  1. Press windows key and click on microsoft store
  2. Click on 3 dots at the corner and select Settings
  3. Turn off Auto Update

4.Stop running Background Apps

  1. Open Settings
  2. Select Privacy
  3. Scroll down to Background Apps
  4. Turn off all non-important apps

5.Turning Off Live tiles

  1. Press windows key and right click on the each tile
  2. Go to More and turn off live tile. Do this for all tiles for better result.
